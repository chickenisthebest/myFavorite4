About

My favorite hobby is creating crafts because I get to express myself in a different way. I also get to relax while doing the crafts and I don't have to worry if the craft is good or not because the craft will be great no matter how it looks. Creating crafts is very enjoyable for me and I feel great after I am finished making crafts. Crafts make me feel like an artist so I do many crafts that feel very awesome to me. Crafting encourages me to experiment in a fun and relaxed environment. I like to have a time of my own just to enjoy what I have and crafts help me have that time of my own. I love thinking like an artist and make different shapes with paper and on paper. This is why creating crafts is my favorite hobby.
